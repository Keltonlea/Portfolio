About Me

Hi! I'm Kelton and I was born in Mountain View, California. In highschool, my family and I moved to Nashville, Tennessee where I finished highschool and went to University of Tennessee, before moving back to San Francisco, California. Currently I live in Bellingham, WA which I am happy to call my home for the last 9 years.

Before becoming a stay at home mom I worked in customer service and product management at an art museum. I decided it was time to learn a new skill and jump into a new field I feel excited about: web development. I'm currently learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, the MERN stack, MYSQL, databases, and git.

In my spare time I like to spend as much time as possible with my daughter , hike, and travel when I can. My other hobbies include exploring museums, video games, paddleboarding, tv, and reading.
